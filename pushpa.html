About the Movie? ▼

Pushpa is an Indian Telugu-language action drama film series created by Sukumar and produced by Mythri Movie Makers, Muttamsetty Media, and Sukumar Writings. The story follows Pushpa Raj, a red sandalwood smuggler who rises through the ranks of a dangerous underworld empire.
